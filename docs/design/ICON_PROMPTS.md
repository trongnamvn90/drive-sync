# DriveSync — Icon Design Prompts

Dùng với Claude + Illustrator để thiết kế app icon.

## Chung cho cả 4 options

- macOS Big Sur+ app icon style (squircle shape)
- 1024x1024px
- 3D-lite: subtle gradients, soft shadows, depth — not flat, not full skeuomorphic
- Color palette:
  - Primary: blue (#2196F3 or #1E88E5)
  - SSD/Drive: silver metallic gradient with subtle reflections
  - Cloud: white with soft opacity
  - Sync arrows: white or light blue
  - Background: blue gradient (dark to light, top-left to bottom-right)
- Clean, modern, professional — should look native next to macOS system apps

---

## Option A: SSD + Sync Arrows

Design a macOS Big Sur+ style app icon (squircle) for a drive sync utility called "DriveSync".

Center element: a portable SSD drive viewed at a 3/4 angle, silver metallic with subtle gradient and reflections. The SSD should be simple and recognizable — rounded rectangle shape, slightly thick, with a small LED indicator dot.

Around the SSD: two curved sync arrows forming a circular loop, wrapping around the drive. The arrows should be white or light blue, with a slight glow effect. They convey continuous synchronization.

Background: blue gradient (#1E88E5 to #42A5F5), dark at top-left, lighter at bottom-right.

Style: 3D-lite with soft shadows, subtle depth. Modern and clean. Should feel like a native macOS system utility icon.

---

## Option B: SSD + Cloud Behind

Design a macOS Big Sur+ style app icon (squircle) for a drive sync utility called "DriveSync".

Foreground: a portable SSD drive viewed at a 3/4 angle, silver metallic with subtle gradient. Positioned slightly lower-center.

Background element: a soft white cloud shape sitting behind and above the SSD, partially visible. The cloud should be semi-transparent with a gentle glow, suggesting cloud storage.

The composition tells the story: physical drive connected to the cloud. The SSD is the main focus, the cloud provides context.

Background: blue gradient (#1E88E5 to #42A5F5), resembling a sky.

Style: 3D-lite with soft shadows. The cloud should feel airy and light, the SSD should feel solid and tangible. Contrast between physical and digital.

---

## Option C: SSD with Light Emanating

Design a macOS Big Sur+ style app icon (squircle) for a drive sync utility called "DriveSync".

Center element: a portable SSD drive viewed at a slight angle, silver metallic. The top edge of the SSD is slightly open/separated, and from the opening, a bright blue-white light beam emanates upward, with subtle light rays and small glowing particles floating up. This suggests data flowing, being alive, actively syncing.

The light creates a dramatic focal point and makes the icon visually striking and unique.

Background: dark blue gradient (#1565C0 to #1E88E5), making the light effect pop.

Style: 3D-lite but slightly more dramatic than typical macOS icons. The light effect should be elegant, not over-the-top — think Spotlight icon vibes.

---

## Option D: SSD + Cloud + Sync Arrows (Combined)

Design a macOS Big Sur+ style app icon (squircle) for a drive sync utility called "DriveSync".

Composition (layered, front to back):
1. Front: a portable SSD drive, silver metallic, 3/4 angle, positioned center-bottom
2. Middle: two curved sync arrows forming a vertical loop between the SSD and the cloud — one arrow going up (upload), one coming down (download). White or light blue with subtle glow.
3. Back: a soft white cloud shape, positioned center-top, partially behind the arrows

The three elements together tell the complete story: drive ↔ sync ↔ cloud. The arrows visually connect the SSD to the cloud.

Background: blue gradient (#1E88E5 to #42A5F5).

Keep it balanced — the SSD and cloud should be similar visual weight, with the arrows as the connecting element. Not too busy despite having 3 elements. Each element should be simple enough to read at smaller sizes.

Style: 3D-lite, soft shadows, modern. Should look cohesive as a single icon, not like 3 separate elements pasted together.
